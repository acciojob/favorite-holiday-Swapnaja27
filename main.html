Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Light and is one of the most visited destinations in the world. It offers a perfect mix of history, art, fashion, and cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to world-famous artworks like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Seine River Cruise – Offers a romantic and scenic view of the city.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A bustling avenue for shopping and culture.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – A buttery and flaky French breakfast pastry.
  • Baguette – A classic French bread that’s crunchy outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate, and sweet treats.
  • Escargots – A traditional French delicacy made with snails.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with either sweet or savory ingredients.
